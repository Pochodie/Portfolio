ATC Simulator

Since July 2024 I have been working on a video game currently named ATC Simulator. It utilizes the XNA framework in .NET via MonoGame. It is starting to grow into quite a large project with over 1,000 hours already invested. It simulates real airports, airplanes, airlines, as well as real weather and fictive passenger lists. It goes without saying that I am composing the soundtrack myself as well! Below are some images from the game!

Solution

Get JSON from  aviationweather.gov.


Piano Concerto

It has always been a big dream to compose a concerto for piano and orchestra. In the summer of 2023 I started making sketches for it and it has now grown to a full scale concert in three movements. Being a professional pianist, working out the piano part is not too challenging, however to make the full instrumentation for an orchestra is quite demanding! Below is some hand-drawn pieces of sheet music from the concerto!

Challenge

Harmony of bar three of the second theme in the first movement not matching with it's counterpart in the recap.

Solution

Change the B♭sus2 to a normal B♭.
