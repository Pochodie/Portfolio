Biography

My name is Daniel Stagno and I am 43 years old. I studied piano for ten years at university level to become a professional concert pianist. I started my professional studies at Malmö music conservatory where I studied under the guidance of Prof. Hans Pålsson. After graduating in 2007 to earn my first master's degree, I continued my studies at the Sibelius-Academy in Helsinki where I studied under the guidance of Hamsa Al-Wadi Juris. During 2009-2010 I was an Erasmus student at the swiss italian conservatory in Lugano where I studied piano and chamber music under the guidance of Alessandro D'Onofrio.

In addition to performing music of the great composers, such as Bach, Beethoven, Chopin and Rachmaninov, I am also composing music on my own. I have composed over 50 songs that have been performed not only in Sweden, but in many other European countries as well as in the United States.
At the moment I am working on a concerto for piano and orchestra as well as a set of variations based on the famous Swedish christmas song Tre Pepparkaksgubbar by Alice Tegnér.

Last year I became increasingly interested in programming and I am currently working on a video game simulating ATC(Air Traffic Control). This game is being made using the MonoGame framework in C# and it's a very challenging project for a single developer! After working almost 1000 hours on the project, I decided to take an introductory course in C# at Luleå University of Technology. I was able to finish the course in less than one month, but even though the assignments felt quite easy I learned many things during the course.

At the moment I am studing an introductory course in JavaScript at Kristianstad University. Having worked only occassionally with HTML and CSS, this course is much more challening!

Using the links on this site you will be able to listen to some of my music as well as to read more about my current projects!
